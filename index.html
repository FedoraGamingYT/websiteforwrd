About us

Welcome to the official website of the Wasteland Research Division, a distinguished faction operating within the Day of Dusk border. Our esteemed organization specializes in the development and distribution of a wide range of essential resources, including batteries, curative solutions, infection guns, and more. These resources are made available for purchase or are supplied to our esteemed allies through our exclusive ally package.

We cordially invite individuals to join our esteemed faction, as we are actively seeking new members to contribute to our collective mission. As a testament to our commitment to excellence, we offer competitive compensation in the form of ROBUX for our automated careers, ensuring prompt payment upon the completion of each order.

To embark on this exciting journey with us, we kindly encourage interested individuals to join our vibrant community on Discord.

Services

At the Wasteland Research Division, we are pioneers in the post-apocalyptic landscape, dedicated to survival, innovation, and collaboration. Discover our comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the unique challenges of the Day of Dusk border. From resource production and distribution to our exclusive Ally Package, our offerings are designed to empower and support our allies in their quest for survival.

Ally Package

Gain access to exclusive benefits and resources through our Ally Package. As a valued ally, you'll receive priority access to our cutting-edge developments, ensuring you stay one step ahead in the post-apocalyptic wasteland. Collaborate with us to strengthen your position and enhance your survival strategies.

Customizable Orders

You can order specific services by becoming a ally of Wasteland Research, if you become a ally you are eligible for our services.

Protection Services

Do you keep getting spawnkilled, randomkilled or you just like getting some protection to not die? You can easily get this service if you have ordered this package. You can have a maximum of 3 users. This service is included in the ally package.

Delivery Services

Are you in the ARD labs but you don't have a battery or you are missing something? Do not worry, WRD will deliver whatever you need wherever you need. Incase of a failed delivery we will insure the item being delivered and give you a new one. 
This service is included in the ally package.

Curing Services

Are you sick but ARD doesn't cure you or you keep getting infected? We do quarantining and curing of your faction incase of a infection, and we can also cure you wherever you require. This service is included in the ally package.
